Right! AS-IS mapping documents how work actually flows through a system today, while TO-BE mapping designs the improved future state process.

4. What is the formula for calculating Takt Time?

Perfect! Takt Time = Available work time ÷ Customer demand. This sets the rhythm for process design and determines required capacity for each step.
