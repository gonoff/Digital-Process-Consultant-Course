6. During the Executive Interview phase, which question type is MOST important for positioning follow-on engagements?

Correct Answer: B
Strategic context questions help position follow-on engagements by connecting operational improvements to business priorities that executives care about most.

Correct Answer: B
Mid-market companies (50-500 employees) should be priced in the $4,000-$7,500 range, balancing value delivered with budget constraints typical for this segment.

8. Which deliverable from the Process X-Ray provides the MOST immediate standalone value to clients?

Correct Answer: B
