1. According to McKinsey & Company studies mentioned in the chapter, what percentage of organizational changes fail to sustain results beyond 18 months without structured support?

Correct Answer: C
McKinsey & Company studies show 70% of organizational changes fail to sustain results beyond 18 months without structured support systems, highlighting the critical importance of post-implementation support.

2. In the Implementation Adoption Curve, which phase typically occurs during months 4-8 after implementation?

Correct Answer: B
The Reality Phase (months 4-8) is when day-to-day pressures return, competing priorities emerge, and initial enthusiasm wanes, potentially causing performance decline from peak levels.

Correct Answer: "Critical" or "Red"
