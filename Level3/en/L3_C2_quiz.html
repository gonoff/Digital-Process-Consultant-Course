Question 1: ADKAR Components

What does the "R" in ADKAR stand for?

  • Results
  • Reinforcement
  • Resources
  • Requirements
Correct Answer: Reinforcement. The R in ADKAR stands for Reinforcement - sustaining the change to prevent reverting to old behaviors.

Question 2: Kotter's 8-Step Process

According to Kotter, what is the FIRST step in successful change management?

  • Form a powerful coalition
  • Create a vision for change
  • Create urgency
  • Communicate the vision
Correct Answer: Create urgency. Step 1 is creating urgency around the need for change to motivate people to act.

Question 3: Change Resistance Scenario

Sarah, a 15-year employee at MedCare Clinic, refuses to use the new EHR system, saying "The old paper charts worked fine for decades." She's influencing other staff to resist the change.

Which ADKAR element is Sarah lacking MOST?

  • Awareness of why change is needed
  • Desire to participate in the change
  • Knowledge of how to change
  • Ability to implement the change
Correct Answer: Desire to participate in the change. Sarah understands the change but doesn't want to embrace it, showing lack of Desire.

Question 4: Change Champion Selection

When selecting change champions for a digital transformation, which characteristic is MOST important?

  • Highest technical skills
  • Formal leadership position
  • Informal influence and credibility
  • Longest tenure with company
Correct Answer: Informal influence and credibility. Change champions are most effective when they have peer respect and influence, regardless of formal position.

Question 5: Communication Frequency

Research shows that people need to hear a message how many times before they internalize it?

  • 3-4 times
  • 5-6 times
  • 7-8 times
  • 10+ times
Correct Answer: 7-8 times. The "Rule of 7" in communication suggests people need to hear a message 7-8 times before it sticks.

Question 10: Real-World Application

AutoFix Garage is implementing a new customer management system. The owner notices that older mechanics are struggling with the technology while younger staff are adapting quickly. Productivity has dropped 15% in the first month.

Which change management action should be the IMMEDIATE priority?

  • Provide additional technical training
  • Replace resistant employees
  • Revert to the old system
  • Create peer mentoring pairs
Correct Answer: Create peer mentoring pairs. This addresses the Ability gap by leveraging the Knowledge of younger staff to help older mechanics.
